Installation

 
BookingWizz for Wordpress (Bookingwizz_wp)  is an addon that allows you to run BookingWizz on your Wordpress site. This addon is a separate purchase, and IS NOT a part of the BookingWizz script.
 
 
BEFORE YOU INSTALL BOOKINGWIZZ FOR WORDPRESS - If you’ve been using
BookingWizz for some time and you have just decided to purchase and install the
Wordpress addon, please be aware that your BookingWizz program will be switched from its
original database to the new wordpress database, and your bookings and settings will not
automatically transfer over to the new database. This will make it seem as if you’ve lost all
your bookings and settings, but this isn’t true.
 
In this case, there will be two (2) options: You can either 1) manually move all bs_* tables from
the standalone database to the new database where your wordpress is installed, or 2) change
the  /booking/includes/dbconnect.php database connection manually to continue using the
standalone database instead of the new wordpress database. Of these two, Option #1 is
preferred way.
 
Downloading BookingWizz for WordPress Addon
First, download “BookingWizz for WordPress” addon from CodeCanyon to  to your desktop:
 
 
 
Unzip the Downloaded Addon
Now, unzip the downloaded addon by double-clicking it, then find the Bookingwizz_wp folder inside.
Drag this folder to your desktop.
 
 
Now open your FTP files. Remember, in our case, we have used the folder called “Treasure.” (if you don't remember, or haven't read how to access your FTP - click here)
 
Double-click on your folder (Treasure, in this case) to find its contents. You should see the
original “booking” folder that you uploaded earlier.
 
 
Upload “bookingwizz_wp” to your site’s plugin folder
This time, however, you are going to upload your Bookingwizz_WP folder into the “Plugins”
folder. First, double-click on the wp-content folder. There you will see the “Plugins” folder.
 
 
 
Double-click on the “Plugins” folder, where you will see all the plugins that are currently installed
on your site, like the example below. Now all you need to do is drag your “bookingwizz_wp”
folder from your desktop into your “Plugins” folder via your FTP.
 
 
 
Activate “bookingwizz_wp” via your site’s admin panel
Now you are ready to activate your “bookingwizz_wp” plugin via your wordpress site’s admin panel.
IMPORTANT:   During wordpress plugin activation, you will be asked to insert your license
key (which is the same as Item Purchase Code you received from Envato after purchasing our product).
 
DO NOT USE THE “BookingWizz WORDPRESS LICENSE KEY” because it will give you
an error message saying that the license key belongs to different product.
 
ONLY USE THE ORIGINAL BookingWizz v5.5
LICENSE AND YOUR CODECANYON USERNAME.
If you use the Licensee’s name from license certificate
instead of YOUR CODECANYON USERNAME, you will
get an "invalid license code" error message.
 
You can find all of this information in your CodeCanyon downloads page. Here’s an image
showing where you can find your license:
 
 
When you click on “License certificate & purchase code”, a document will pop up that you
should open to find your License Key (= item purchase code).
